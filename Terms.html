1. Use of the App

You may use Swap-Up only if you are an employee or manager of an organisation using the app, provide accurate information, and follow workplace rules. Accounts misusing the app may be suspended.

You are responsible for keeping login details secure, not sharing your account, reporting suspicious activity, and ensuring GPS accuracy during check-in/out. Misuse may result in account action.
